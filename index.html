About Us

Coach Kevin Robertson

Having started running in 2015, I have spent the last ten years honing my running skills, and I now want to put them to good use by offering personalized support to help you achieve your goals. As an athlete, I am a middle-distance runner, specializing in the 800m to 5000m. I have personal bests of 2:19.52 in the 1000m, 3:38.33 in the 1500m, 7:47.53 in the 3000m, and 13:38.80 in the 5000m. I have also represented Canada four times on the international stage. I am curious by nature and like to stay up to date on the latest training methods. I am also the informal creator of the famous “jogvalles.” Outside of running, I have been working as a lawyer since 2022, which has led me to become precise and meticulous not only at work but also in sports and coaching.

Coach Matthew Beaudet

I have been coaching since 2019, beginning at the high school and junior club level in middle distance, distance, cross country and subsequently progressing to personal coaching for road races, mainly the 5k to Marathon. My favourite thing about coaching is seeing athletes achieve results that they did not think they would have been able to achieve after working hard and pushing themselves in training ! I am a generally curious person and enjoy reading books and research articles on training, and trying out new methods and techniques in training. I also believe frequent communication and feedback is key in coaching and always want to stay up to date with athletes' training, feelings and progress. As an athlete, I am a 1500 (3:37), 3000m (7:43) 5000m (13:48) and 10km (29:43) runner. I have represented Canada at the World Cross Country Championships and Quebec at the Canada Games, and have two U23 Quebec records, in the Mile and 5000m. I recently completed my university degree in electrical engineering at McGill where I ran Track and XC, winning Athlete of the Year, Rookie of the Years and multiple medals on both the national and provincial circuit, and currently work as a candidate to the engineering profession (CEP), which helps me bring a problem solving approach to coaching.

Coaching Services

  • 1-on-1 Coaching
  • Customized Training Plans for any distance, 800m-Marathon. Based on science and proven training methods, adapted to your everyday schedule, past-injuries and motivation.
  • Race Preparation, Nutrition Planning & Strategy

How We Work

  • You book a 30-45 minute call with us to discuss your goals, previous training and injuries, availibilities and other pertinent information.
  • You receive a training plan based on this discussion every 4 weeks for 50$/plan.
  • Over the 4 weeks, we check in and you give us feedback on the training by text or phone call, and we send a new plan for the next block.
  • Time and location permitting, we come and supervise key training sessions.
